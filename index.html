The Problem

Dehydration is invisible —
until it's too late.

Current tools either replace equipment athletes love, violate sponsorship deals, or infer hydration from sweat. None measure actual fluid intake. Coaches operate completely blind.

🦴

Smart Bottles Don't Work

Athletes have their bottle. Smart bottles violate Gatorade/Powerade sponsorships and create adoption friction no program wants.

⌚

Wearables Only Guess

WHOOP, Garmin, Oura — all infer hydration from sweat. No direct measurement. No real-time intervention capability.

📋

Manual Tracking Fails

Compliance is near zero. Coaches see only lagging indicators — after the damage is done and the injury has happened.

53%

of NCAA Division I athletes begin practice already hypohydrated — before they've even broken a sweat.

10-20%

performance degradation from dehydration alone. More than most injuries.

Wardenaar et al., 2016 · Deloitte, 2024

How It Works

Attach. Drink. Know.

No behavior change. No bottle replacement. syp clips onto any water bottle and gives you — and your entire team — real-time, verified hydration data.

01
Hardware

Universal Attachment

syp's patent-pending load cell sleeve attaches to any water bottle in seconds. Works with existing equipment. No sponsorship conflicts. Zero athlete resistance.

02
Software

Real-Time Measurement

Direct fluid intake measurement syncs automatically via Bluetooth. Personalized targets based on body metrics, activity, and climate — not sweat-based guesses.

03
Intelligence

Team Visibility

Coaches see the full team on a live dashboard. At-risk alerts fire before performance degrades. Real data. Real decisions. Zero guesswork.
